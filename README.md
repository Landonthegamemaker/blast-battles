# Blast Battle

Surviving collapse and rebuilding hope one battle at a time. Blast Battle is a fast-paced, simple, card-based battle royale game where heroes, villains, and legends collide. After a rogue AI turns against its creators, The Great Collapse occurs. You must battle bots across zones to protect what remains of humanity, and discover a new way forward.
