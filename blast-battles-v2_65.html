HOW TO PLAY

PHASES: Each turn has 4 combat phases: Fast → Medium → Slow → Charged. You can move OR play a card each phase — not both.
MOVEMENT: Click any highlighted tile to move (up to 1 space; Swift characters move 2). Moving counts as your action and ends your phase — choose wisely!
CARDS: Select a card from your hand, then click FIRE or EQUIP. Defense cards can be played in any phase.
RANGE: Melee=0, Pistol/Shotgun/Grenade=1, AR/Missile=2, Sniper=3. Distance = shortest path on the 5×5 grid.
ARENA: 5×5 grid — player starts top-left, bot starts bottom-right. Snipers are now viable at max range!
ARMOR: Reduces damage; specialized armor is more effective. Durability drops with each hit.
TIMER: Each phase has a 20-second countdown at 1x speed. Use the PHASE SPEED slider to control auto-skip: 1x = full 20s, 5x = auto-skip after 4s if no cards are playable. Higher speed = faster gameplay.
LOCATIONS: Hero Zone buffs heroes, Villain Zone buffs villains, Armory draws a weapon, Forge draws armor, Radiation deals 8 dmg/turn, Scrap Heap discards a card.
VICTORY: Reduce the opponent's HP to 0, or survive 20 turns with more HP than your opponent to win the battle. Do you have what it takes to take on the bots?
